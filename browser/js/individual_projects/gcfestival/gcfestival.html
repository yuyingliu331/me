Global Citizen Festival 2014

The Global Citizen Festival is an annual music festival that brings together global leaders and artists to make contributions towards ending extreme poverty.

Tickets can be won by taking a series of actions and earning points.

With the help of Global Citizen's in house graphic designer, I created this gameboard design. The gameboard was used to encourage users to take actions in a specified order. It saw over 50,000 visitors daily in the three months leading up to the Festival.

The page featured clickable actions with hover-over information for each one. It worked across browsers, and was mobile friendly.
